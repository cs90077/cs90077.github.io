開啟相機拍照

step 1 - 設定權限

info.plist
1Privacy - Camera Usage Description

step 2 - 開啟相機

1func openCamera() {
2    let pickerController = UIImagePickerController()
3    pickerController.delegate = self
4    pickerController.mediaTypes = ["public.image"]
5    pickerController.sourceType = .camera
6    self.present(pickerController, animated: true, completion: nil)
7}

如果要多錄影功能，請添加 public.movie 屬性

1pickerController.mediaTypes = ["public.image", "public.movie"]

效果
