TableView下拉更新資料

目錄

作法

iOS 10之後，UITableView支援內建的refreshControl

step 1

使用UIRefreshControl

1let refreshControl = UIRefreshControl()

step 2

把UIRefreshControl設定到UITableView上

1 // refresh
2refreshControl.addTarget(self, action: #selector(self.refreshData), for: .valueChanged)
3refreshControl.attributedTitle = NSAttributedString(string:"更新資料")
4tableView.refreshControl = refreshControl

step 3

設定事件這邊要做重新更新資料

1@objc func refreshData() {
2    dataSource = [["1","2","3","3","3"],["1","2","3","4","5"]]
3    self.tableView.reloadData()
4    self.refreshControl.endRefreshing()
5}
